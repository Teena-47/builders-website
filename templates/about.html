About Sterling Builders

Our Story

Founded in 2006, Sterling Builders began as a small residential contracting outfit in Bengaluru and has since grown into a full-scale construction company handling residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects across India.

We believe great construction is a mix of engineering precision and genuine care for the people who will live and work in what we build. That philosophy has guided every project we've delivered — over 120 of them to date.
